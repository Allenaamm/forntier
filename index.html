Using Frontier Mail on Mobile Devices

You can also access Frontier Email using your smartphone or tablet by configuring your email app (like Gmail or Outlook) with these settings:

  • Incoming Mail Server (IMAP): imap.frontier.com
  • Outgoing Mail Server (SMTP): smtp.frontier.com
  • Port (IMAP): 993 with SSL
  • Port (SMTP): 465 with SSL

Troubleshooting Tips

  • Ensure your username and password are correct.
  • Clear your browser cache or try using a different browser.
  • Check for service outages on the Frontier website.
  • Disable any browser extensions that may block the login process.
